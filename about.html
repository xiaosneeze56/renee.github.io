Service. There is no better word to describe what I love to do.

I consider myself to be a service-oriented person, and I aspire to be an engineer who creates a better world. In the past, I have enjoyed using my skills (e.g. CAD modeling, Audacity, Photoshop, Microsoft Office, public speaking, etc.) to serve my local community.

In my (almost non-existent) free time, you can find me experimenting with VPython, learning a new computer language, constructing something new with Autodesk Fusion, tutoring students in math, running, or reading an Agatha Christie mystery/history book.

If you'd like to get in touch with me, please do not hesitate to send me an email!
